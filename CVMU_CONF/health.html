Health Science (Pharmacy)

About Track Sub Track Theme
Pharmaceutical sciences combine a broad range of scientific disciplines — including medicinal chemistry, analytical chemistry, structural biology, biopharmaceutics, pharmacokinetics, pharmacology, and toxicology — that are critical to the discovery and development of new drugs and therapies. The scope of research in pharmaceutical sciences ranges from identifying new drug targets and therapeutic agents to delivery and repurposing of drugs in clinical settings.
